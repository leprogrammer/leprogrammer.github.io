About Me

Some of my interests include teaching, working with robots, learning new programming languages, and playing soccer or card games.

Experience

kidOYO Mentor   |   September 2016 - Present

I joined the Noizivy organization in September 2016 as an intern and had the opportunity of taking on the unique challenge of teaching programming languages to children aged 8 to 16. Starting in the spring semester of 2017, I moved up from an intern and took up a position as a mentor for the kidOYO program.
